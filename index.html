ABOUT CONTACT












Lamborghini is an Italian manufacturer of high-performance luxury sports cars and SUVs, known for their cutting-edge design, powerful engines, and advanced technology, including a focus on hybrid electrification and sustainability through its "Direzione Cor Tauri" strategy. Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini, the company is now a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group through Audi AG and produces models like the Revuelto, Urus, and Temerario, each embodying the brand's DNA of performance and emotion.
